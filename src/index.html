About me

I have been programming for more than 5 years. I started with mobile app development using the Flutter framework and then moved on to web application development using Vue.js and React.js. I worked on that for about a year, but then I was attracted to native iOS development. I have been doing iOS development for more than 2 years. During that time, I had the opportunity to work on several mobile applications (both larger and smaller) and learned a lot of things.

In my free time, I try to improve my development skills by watching courses, videos, or reading blogs. I like to expand my horizons in other areas of development besides iOS. I also enjoy backend work, where I have worked with frameworks like Laravel or Vapor Swift.

When I'm not programming, I like to watch F1 races and also regularly play hockey.
