Privacy Policy – Photo to PDF: Quick Converter

Effective Date: September 5, 2025

We respect your privacy. This app (“Photo to PDF: Quick Converter”) is designed to process all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information.

Data Processing

  • All photos and documents are processed entirely on your device.
  • No images, PDFs, or other personal files are uploaded to any server.

Permissions

  • Camera Access: Used only if you choose to take a photo to convert it into a PDF. The app does not record or store your photos outside of your device.
  • Photo Library Access: Used only to allow you to select photos to convert into PDFs.

Purchases

  • The app offers in-app purchases and subscriptions. Payment information is handled securely by Apple and we do not have access to it.

Third-Party Services

  • This app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection.
